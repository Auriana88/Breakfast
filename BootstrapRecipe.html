Fruit and Granola Parfait

by: Auriana Hollister

One would make this at any time of day. A Fruit and Yogurt Parfait is a healthy alternative and very fulfilling. I make this, particulary at breakfast, to give me the right amount of energy throughout the day.

Ingredients
  • Strawberries (or your choice of fruit)
  • Vanilla Yogurt
  • Chocolate and Red Berry Granola (or your choice of granola)
  • Honey (For EXTRA sweetness)

What to do
  1. Cut up your choice of fruit
  2. Scoop desired amount of yogurt in a bowl
  3. Place desired fruit on top
  4. Add a drizzle of honey ( optional )
  5. Add more yogurt
  6. Add more fruit
  7. Layer your choice of granola
  8. Drizzle more honey on top
  9. ENJOY
